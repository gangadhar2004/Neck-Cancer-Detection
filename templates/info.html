Types of Neck Cancer

Oral Cavity Cancer

Description: Cancer that forms in the tissues of the oral cavity (mouth).

Symptoms: Persistent mouth sores, lumps, pain while chewing, etc.

Pharyngeal Cancer

Description: Cancer that forms in the pharynx (throat).

Symptoms: Sore throat, difficulty swallowing, voice changes, etc.

Laryngeal Cancer

Description: Cancer that forms in the larynx (voice box).

Symptoms: Hoarseness, difficulty swallowing, ear pain, etc.

Nasopharyngeal Cancer

Description: Cancer that forms in the nasopharynx, the upper part of the throat behind the nose.

Symptoms: Nasal congestion, nosebleeds, hearing loss, etc.

Hypopharyngeal Cancer

Description: Cancer that forms in the hypopharynx, the bottom part of the throat.

Symptoms: Sore throat, ear pain, difficulty breathing, etc.

Salivary Gland Cancer

Description: Cancer that forms in the salivary glands.

Symptoms: Lump near the jaw or neck, numbness, facial pain, etc.
